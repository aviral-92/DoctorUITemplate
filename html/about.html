We are the young group of engineer. We want to provide world's best platform for the people of India & rest of the world to use medical facilities.We wants to connect rural areas of our country to best of medical facilities available near to the area so that in case of emergency they no where to go & how fast they can reach the best doctors near by them. We aren't multi-billionaire people. We don't wish to have competition with anyone. We have created this site aaspaasdoctor.com because in case of emergency our first question in hindi is "koi doctor hai aaspaas" means is their any doctor available near by. Almost we all have encountered this situation in our life. Our platform aaspaasdoctor.com will connect each individual to nearby medical facilities and it will save many life's as well as time of many person.
